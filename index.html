Roadmap

Testy Tigers Quiz
(WL System)

Tiger Quiz is an innovative app that will allows us and other projects to allocate users wallet addresses in a new and unique way. The app requires users to read the whitepaper and take a quiz, which tests their understanding of the project. Those who fail the quiz are given the opportunity to try again in 24 hours. If a user fails the quiz three times, they will be rejected from participating in the mint.

Testy Tigers Launchpad

We will be utilizing our own launchpad for the minting process using the Metaplex candy machine 3. The price and launch date have yet to be determined. For further information, please join our Discord server and stay updated through our announcements channel.

Tiger Stance: Change Traits form

Our Stance platform allows you to modify certain features of your tiger, such as its clothing, headgear, or eyewear, without altering its rarity or metadata. You can switch between these modified forms and the original form at will.

Twin Female Tiger

Each tiger from our NFT collection has a twin sister, a female tiger with the same traits. The airdrop will be 1 week after the launch and the ability to replace your Male tiger will begin after 3-5 weeks after the launch. Only some of the holders will be able to replace their Male tigers with their Twin female tigers, only the ones who are non-listed or staked during a snapshot will happen at a random time. You will always have the ability to replace your tiger from male to female or vice versa.

Staking and $TAIL token

We will be launching our Staking platform post mint, where holders will have the opportunity to earn $tail tokens by participating in staking. These tokens will serve as a means of exploring the Testy Tigers universe and will provide real utility to holders. Our team is working with experts in tokenomics to ensure that the $tail token has a solid foundation and is not just a speculative instrument.

Project Pipelines

Pipelines aims to help Testy Tigers and other NFT projects investing in their communities by rewarding contributors such as artists, meme makers, video editors, illustrators, and musicians... through a voting system. Up to 50% of Testy Tiger royalties will be invested in the community contributors using the Pipelines platform.

Gaming Exploration

Our team is eager to conduct research and develop games for the community and the gaming industry as a whole. We are open to exploring new possibilities within the gaming industry.

How do I get whitelisted?

To obtain a whitelist for Testy Tigers, you will need to pass the Tiger Quiz with a score of 80% or higher. If your score falls between 50% and 79%, your application will be placed under review, and if your score is less than 50%, you will have to wait 24 hours before retaking the quiz.
To prepare for the quiz, it is crucial to study the roadmap thoroughly. During the quiz, read each question carefully and take your time selecting the correct answer.

Keep in mind that you have three attempts to pass the quiz before your application is rejected.
